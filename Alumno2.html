Cartoons

I remember that the cartoons were simpler, the chapters had a beginning and an end, they had a main villain and a protagonist or group of protagonists, the colors they used were not bright and they had a large number of chapters. The cartoons were drawn by sketches on paper.

Cartoons

At present the cartoons are more shiny, the chapters are more complicated and you need to see past seasons to understand them, there are plot twists in the history of the protagonists and villains. Another change is in the use of CGI for animation which is cheaper but not all people like it.

Music

Music in the past used instruments and the singer did not have so many modifications in his voice, the musical genres were varied and each one could be distinguished.

Music

At present, music uses a lot of computer produced instruments, singers use more voice modifiers and the dominant genres are electronic and reggeton.

Video game guides

Video game guides have undergone changes, in the past you needed to buy a magazine to review a guide or some command, sometimes those guides or commands were false.

Video game guides

At present, the guides are easier to get, you can buy a magazine or look for a guide on the internet, either in a blog or in a YouTube video, they are usually real guides.
