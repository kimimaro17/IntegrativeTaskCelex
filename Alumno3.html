Video game consoles

The consoles of before compared to those of now are very different mainly because of their accessories such as controls, chargers, etc.

Video game consoles

Televisions

The televisions have also changed, before they were like big and heavy boxes that needed an antenna like rabbit ears, they had wheels to go up and change the channel, also a lot of static.

Televisions

The televisions of now are flat, they are made of LED, less static, the antennas are parabolic, they have internet access, and streaming platforms.

Phones

Something that has changed over time is that telephones used to be very simple, they could only be used for calls or messages, and there was infrared.

Phones

Now the phones have many functions calls, messages, alarms, video calls, photos, internet connection, apps and many more functions.
